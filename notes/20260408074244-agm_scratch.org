** Headline Targets

| Goal              | Current   | Target         |

| Revenue           | Rs.286L     | Rs.572L+ (2×)    |
| Gross Margin      | 5–6%      | 10%            |

| Small Ticket TAT  | —         | =<7 Days        |

** 6-Step Action Plan

*** Step 1 — Pipeline & Closure Ratio Audit
Analyse current deal flow; identify conversion bottlenecks by stage.
- Map every deal stage from cold call to closure
- Calculate drop-off % at each stage
- KPI: Stage-wise conversion report produced within 30 days

*** Step 2 — Drop Low-Probability Deals Fast
Score deals with <30% turnaround probability → exit decision within =<3 days
to free up team bandwidth for higher-value opportunities.
- KPI: Zero deals sitting >7 days with <30% probability score

*** Step 3 — TAT Reduction: 21 → =<11 Days
- Define stage-level SLAs (e.g., first visit within 3 days, negotiation close within 5 days)
- Escalate stuck deals at the 7-day mark
- KPI: Avg. TAT measured fortnightly; target =<11 days by Q2

*** Step 4 — High-Revenue Deal Focus
Prioritise deals >=Rs.20L; same team effort, 2× revenue per closure.
- KPI: % of pipeline >=Rs.20L to increase from current mix to 50%+

*** Step 5 — Margin Push via Book & Sell Model
Shift deal mix to 60% Book & Sell to achieve 10% average margin.
- Current margin: 5–6% (brokerage-heavy mix)
- Book & Sell adds 4–5% incremental margin per deal
- KPI: Book & Sell as % of closed deals tracked monthly

*** Step 6 — Fast-Track Small Tickets (<Rs.5L)
Delegate & close within =<7 days; reduce senior bandwidth drain.
- Create a delegation SOP for junior team members
- KPI: All <Rs.5L deals closed within 7 days; breach flagged next day

* Slide 05 — Mentorship & Team Building
:PROPERTIES:
:DESCRIPTION: Building a high-agency team that operates with autonomy, speed, and precision
:END:

** 1. Nurture Leaders
Build a team of high-agency individuals who own outcomes, not just tasks.
- Monthly 1:1 reviews tied to individual deal KPIs
- Encourage team members to bring deal strategies, not just problems

** 2. Equip with the Right Tools
Provide CRM access, pipeline dashboards, and deal-tracking templates
so the team focuses on selling, not admin.
- Tools: CRM (pipeline view), Excel dashboard, WhatsApp Business, mail templates
- Target: Zero manual follow-up tracking by end of Q2

** 3. Impart a Tech-Savvy Culture
Train team on productivity tools to eliminate mundane tasks:
- Excel dashboards for pipeline visibility
- Mail automation for follow-up sequences
- WhatsApp Business for customer communication tracking

** 4. How We Measure Team Performance
Each team member reviewed fortnightly on:
- Deals owned (pipeline size)
- Conversion % (leads → closures)
- Average TAT per deal
- Margin per deal
- Target achievement % vs monthly goal
