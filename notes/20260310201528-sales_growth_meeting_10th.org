** Trekking & Alex Honnold Mindset – Applied to Sales (4 min)
*** Key Ideas from Honnold / Trekking
- Not fearlessness, but *preparation + exposure*:
  - Big climbs become “low‑risk” in his mind because of practice and rehearsal.
- Incremental goals:
  - Huge achievements = many small, daily goals written in a journal.
- Intentional risk:
  - “Everyone is going to die; better to take calculated risks you choose than random risks of inaction.”

*** How This Applies to Our Sales Work
- Difficult deals = big wall:
  - Break them into steps:
    - Today: get requirements clearly.
    - Next: share 2–3 options.
    - Next: do one deep CDM search and one follow‑up call.
- Exposure therapy to rejection:
  - Many calls → many “No’s” → gradually you become calmer.
  - Treat each “No” like one more move on the wall; you keep going.

- Intrinsic motivation:
  - Focus on *Art of selling*:
    - Better questions,
    - Better matching of machines,
    - Cleaner WhatsApp communication.
