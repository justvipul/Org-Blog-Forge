#+title: Chamar Leni – 1 Nov 2025
- Region: Near Nashik, accessible via Peth road side; cave complex on a hill.[web:12][web:15]
- Grade: Easy–Moderate; mix of steps, path and some steeper bits.
- Route: Base near road → steps/path up → caves and temple structures → further path to top plateau area used by trekkers for practice.
- Trek time: 2–3 hrs overall including exploring caves.
- Access: From Nashik via Peth road; used often as a practice trek for tougher climbs like Pandav Leni rounds.
- Best season: Year-round, but post-monsoon and winter are most pleasant.
- Water/food: Carry your own; occasional local stalls near base depending on day.
- Safety: Watch steps when crowded or wet; carry a headlamp if exploring darker cave corners.
- Fact: The site has religious and historic significance, and for you it also became a “training ground” after Pandav Leni.
- Quote: “Chamar Leni is where rock, faith and fitness quietly shake hands.”
