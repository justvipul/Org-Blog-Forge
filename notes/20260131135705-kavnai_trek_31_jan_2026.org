* Trek Overview
1. Trek Name: Kavnai Fort
2. Region: Igatpuri
3. Range: Igatpuri range, Sahyadri Sub-range
4. Base Village: Kavnai (250 houses near Ghoti)
5. Height (ft): 2998 ft. from M.S.L
6. Type: Hill fort
7. Difficulty Level: Easy
8. Best Season: Winter, Summer, Rainy
9. Time to Climb: 2 hours / half day trek
10. Water Availability: Not suitable for drinking
11. Suitable For: Beginners

* Route Information
1. Route from Base City: Nashik → Ghoti Road → turn right to Vaitarnadam Road → from Waaki village 1.5km turn right → Kavnai Gaon (4.5km after) [Total: 40 km from Nashik]
2. Final Village Distance: 40 km from Nashik
3. Public Transport Availability: Auto repair at Ghoti, Petrol at Ghoti & Igatpuri
4. Parking / Auto / Petrol: Available at Ghoti and Igatpuri

* Historical / Mythological Background
1. Historical Importance: राज्याभिषेक सोहळ्यानंतर (स्वराज्य) स्थापन झाले. श्रद्धा आहे की शिवाजी महाराजांनी गुप्तपणे भेट दिली.
2. Legends / Myths: रामायण काळात हनुमानाने कालनेमी राक्षसाचा वध केला म्हणून पर्वताला हे नाव
3. Nearby Heritage / Religious Sites:
   - Kapiladhar Tirth (ज्ञानेश्वर व गजानन महाराज पदस्पर्श)
   - Kapildhara Gajanan Maharaj Mandir

* Key Landmarks & Attractions
1. Temples (ALL ON TOP):
   - *Kamakshi Devi Mandir* (main temple by lake)
   - *Chamunda Ma Temple* (met priest)
   - *Shiva Temple with Nandi* (by lake) 
   - Kapildhara Gajanan Maharaj Mandir (base)
2. Structures (Bastions, Caves, Gates, etc.):
   - North entrance door
   - Small caves (right side at top)
   - Bastion on west side
   - Buruj and historical remains (south side)
   - Water tank
   - Iron ladder at entrance for convenient climbing
3. Viewpoints (Ranges / Dams visible):
   - Kalsubai range
   - Trimbak range  
   - Tringalwadi and other forts
   - Vaitarnadam / Mukne Dam (rays reflecting in clear water)
4. Not to Miss:
   - *Lake on top* - Temple & cloud reflections + school of pet fishes
   - Chamunda Ma Temple (met priest)
   - Shiva temple by lake

* Experience & Observations
1. Weather / Terrain: Cool breeze over massive fort
2. Notable Sights: Chamunda Mandir, Shiva temple, Kamakshi Mandir, lake ALL on top. Lake has school of pet fish (rohu/catla varieties).
3. Personal Experience: 3 dogs accompanied entire trek. Small picnic with roti + potato sabji at top. Prasad at Kapildhara: 2 roti, rice, pithla, gilka sabji
4. Vibe / Mood of Place: Peaceful, spiritual, mesmerizing reflections
5. Tips for Future Trekkers: Carry own water, visit Kapildhara for prasadam (10 AM-6:30 PM)

* Facilities & Amenities
1. Food / Water Availability: No facilities at fort. Kapildhara Gajanan Maharaj: prasadam 10 AM-6:30 PM (2 roti, rice, pithla, gilka sabji)
2. Accommodation / Prasadam Timings: Kapildhara prasadam daily 10 AM-6:30 PM
3. Shops / Auto Repair / Petrol Info: Auto repair & petrol at Ghoti
4. Guide Needed: No
