  6. [ ] (why?)
  7. Region: Near Raigad Nagar / RPF firing range belt outside Nashik.
  8. Grade: Easy; intentionally kept as a short family trek.
  9. Route: Short hill path from selected starting point → small top with dam view (Waldevi side) → back the same route.
  10. Trek time: ~1.5–2 hrs total including enjoying the view.
  11. Access: Close to Nashik; sensitive area due to firing range – exact route details are best kept offline and within trusted groups.
  12. Best season: Clear winter mornings for best dam views.
  13. Water/food: Carry a bottle; breakfast plan was at a Jain temple on your group trek.
  14. Safety: Respect firing range boundaries and all instructions; this is not a random wander hill.
  15. Fact: This tekdi is more about safe, small practice climbs and views than about height or difficulty.
  16. Quote: “Some hills are not about adventure; they are about learning how to move in sensitive places with respect.”
